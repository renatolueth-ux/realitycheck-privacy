🔒 Privacy Policy

RealityCheck is designed with privacy in mind.

📦 Data Storage

All user settings (such as timer duration and interval configurations) are stored locally on your device.

No data is stored on external servers.

🚫 No Data Collection

This app does not collect, track, or share any personal data.

🔔 System Features

The app uses system features such as notifications and alarms to provide its core functionality.

🌐 Offline Usage

RealityCheck works entirely offline.
